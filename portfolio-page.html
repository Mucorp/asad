Kualitee

Kualitee is a test management tool that helps teams organize all their testing on one platform. It also serves as a defect management platform, offering hassle-free bug management through its issue tracking tool. With Kualitee, you can maintain test case repositories, execute test cycles, and log defects. You can also keep track of your testing progress through its customizable dashboard and reports. Kualitee provides everything to agile teams that they require in a software testing life-cycle. It serves as a complete ALM solution, that helps you plan, design, manage and execute software tests faster through intuitive workflows and in a highly collaborative environment. Moreover, you can choose between cloud or on-premise and integrate with a host of other tools.
